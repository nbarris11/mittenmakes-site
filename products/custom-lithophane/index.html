What makes a custom lithophane special

A lithophane turns a photo into a 3D printed image that reveals more detail when light passes through it. It works well as a keepsake because it feels more tactile, personal, and display-worthy than a simple print or frame.

For Mitten Makes, it is also a great match for the brand because it fits the made-to-order, handmade-in-Michigan angle naturally. People usually order a lithophane because they want a gift tied to a specific memory, person, or moment.

Good occasions for a custom lithophane

  • Mother's Day gifts when you want something sentimental and personal.
  • Memorial or keepsake gifts when the photo itself is the point.
  • Anniversaries and family gifts when a regular framed photo feels too expected.
