Creature Requests

If you have a specific creature in mind, this is the right starting point. Mitten Makes can help with custom articulated creature requests even when the exact animal or style is not part of the regular stocked catalog yet.

That means slug requests, axolotl ideas, ocean creatures, fantasy creatures, and other creature-based gifts can all start here. Because everything is handmade and made to order in Metro Detroit, we can talk through the look, colors, size, and quantity before anything is finalized.

Helpful things to include

  • The creature itself. Slug, axolotl, dinosaur, shark, dragon, or something else entirely.
  • The vibe. Cute, realistic, collectible, colorful, simple, or fidget-friendly.
  • How it will be used. Keychain, desk buddy, small gift, display creature, or batch order.
