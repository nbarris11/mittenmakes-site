Decorative Vases

Decorative vases are a clean, giftable way to add custom 3D printed decor to a shelf, table, office, or home corner. These made-to-order pieces start at $20 and are handmade in Metro Detroit with room for different shapes, finishes, and display styles.

Request a decorative vase See all custom options

Who decorative vases are good for

These work well for people shopping for simple home decor, hostess gifts, office desk accents, and little shelf pieces that feel more personal than mass-market decor. They are also a nice fit for gift baskets and seasonal decorating when you want something handmade but easy to style.

Since they are made to order, we can shape the request around the kind of space you are decorating rather than forcing you into a one-style-fits-all listing.

Helpful request details

  • Where it will live. Shelf, entry table, desk, kitchen, or gift table.
  • The look. Minimal, playful, modern, textured, or color-forward.
  • Whether it is decorative or functional. That helps guide the right shape and finish direction.
