Common questions

How long does production take?

Most items ship within 5–7 business days. Larger or custom pieces may take a bit longer. We'll always confirm timing before we start printing.

Can I choose my own colors?

Absolutely. We offer a wide range of colors and finishes, from bright and glossy to soft and subtle. DM us on Instagram and we'll share what is currently available.

Do you ship nationally?

Yes! We ship anywhere in the US. Local pickup in Metro Detroit is also available by arrangement — just mention it when you reach out.

How does payment work?

We confirm the final price with you before anything is due. Local pickup orders can be paid with cash or Venmo, and shipped orders can be paid with Venmo or PayPal after we confirm the details.

Can I request a completely custom design?

Yes, and we love it. Share your idea on Instagram or through the order form, whether it is a one-off gift, a classroom batch, or something practical for your home, and we'll work with you on what's possible.

Are these just for kids?

Not at all. Many pieces make great gifts for kids, but we also create desk accessories, collectibles, and personalized items that adults love too.

What materials do you use?

We use high-quality 3D printing materials chosen for durability, clean detail, and beautiful color. Every piece is printed with care for a polished final result.
