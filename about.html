About Mitten Makes

Mitten Makes started as a 3D printing hobby and grew into something we're genuinely proud of. Based in Metro Detroit, we make every piece to order using high-quality materials and finishes that feel special, look great, and hold up to everyday use — whether it's for a kid, a parent, an adult shopping for something fun, or someone with a very specific idea in mind.

We believe in the craft of making. Each item is personalized just for you because handmade is not just a selling point, it is how we work. No mass production, no shortcuts, just quality prints made with care and room for ideas that are playful, practical, or a little unexpected.

Why Handmade Matters

In a world of quick, disposable things, there's something meaningful about owning something created specifically for you. Made-to-order work means we can take your ideas seriously, use materials we love, and make sure every piece meets our standards before it reaches you, whether that means one custom gift, a batch for a classroom, or a practical print made for a space in your home.

You're not buying inventory. You're ordering directly from makers who care about what they make.

Get in Touch

Have a question? Want to see samples? Ready to order? Find us on Instagram: @mittenmakes. We can help with gifts, classroom requests, custom organizers, local pickup or shipping, and payment by cash, Venmo, or PayPal once your order is confirmed.
