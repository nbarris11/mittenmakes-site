Our Story

Mitten Makes started as a 3D printing hobby and grew into something we're genuinely proud of. Based in Metro Detroit, we make every piece to order using high-quality materials and finishes that feel special, look great, and hold up to everyday use — whether it's for a kid, a parent, or an adult shopping for something fun.

We believe in the craft of making. Each item is personalized just for you—because handmade isn't just a selling point, it's how we work. No mass production, no shortcuts, just quality prints made with care.

Why Handmade Matters

In a world of quick, disposable things, there's something meaningful about owning something created specifically for you. Made-to-order work means we can take your ideas seriously, use materials we love, and make sure every piece meets our standards before it reaches you.

You're not buying inventory. You're ordering directly from makers who care about what they make.
